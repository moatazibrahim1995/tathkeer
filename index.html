FAQ

Is this a full smartwatch?

No. It’s intentionally simple: time + short, two-line prompts + discreet vibrations.

Does it work without my phone?

Yes for core reminders. The app is for setup/updates; prayer-time logic runs on the device (offline).

Water resistance?

Production target is IP67 (dust-tight; short immersion). We’ll share test clips before preorders.

Battery life?

Target is multi-day (≥72 h) under typical nudges. We’ll publish 72-hour logs before preorders.

Privacy?

No cloud needed for core reminders. No ads or tracking. Pair once; your settings stay on the device.
